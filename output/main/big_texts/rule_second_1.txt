AT

When specifying the exact time:
-at one o'clock
-at 9 pm
-at noon

With the word time:
-at the same time
-at bedtime
-at breakfast time
-at dinner time

When specifying the time of day:
at night
-in the morning
-in the afternoon
-in the evening

To indicate age:
-at 18
-at the age of 35

With the names of the holidays:
-at Easter
-at New Yea
-at Christmas
